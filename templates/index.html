Carys' Word Guessing Game

The game will generate a word, you have to guess that word. If the randomly generated word comes after yours in the dictionary, the game will respond higher. If the word comes before yours in the dictionary the game will respond lower. Keep going until you guess the word! The easy dictionary consists of the 100 most common words in the english language, the medium consists of 1000 and the hard dictionary consists of over 30000 words.
